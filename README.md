# Shantanu Mishra — Product Management Portfolio

This repo hosts my product management portfolio as I transition from telecom NOC engineering into Product Management.

🔗 **Live site:** https://therealshub.github.io/portfolio/

## What's here

- `index.html` — One-page portfolio: summary, experience, skills, certifications, and product case studies
- `noc_dashboard_mockup.html` — Interactive wireframe mockup for the NOC Incident Dashboard redesign
- `NOC_Dashboard_Case_Study.docx` — Full write-up of the case study: problem statement, user research, proposed solution, and success metrics

## Background

I spent over a year as an OMCR (Operations & Maintenance Center – Radio) Engineer monitoring live telecom infrastructure for Reliance Jio, working rotating shifts as part of a 5-person NOC team. The first case study in this portfolio is grounded directly in that experience — addressing alert fatigue and shift handoff gaps I observed first-hand on the Zabbix monitoring platform.
